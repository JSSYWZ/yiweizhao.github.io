Understanding Equity Release Mortgages (ERM)

What is an ERM?

An Equity Release Mortgage (ERM) allows homeowners aged 55+ to access property equity without selling their home. Repayment typically occurs when the borrower passes away or moves into long-term care.

Why Longevity Matters

  • 🔍 Determines interest compounding period
  • ⚖️ Balances lender risk vs borrower benefit
  • 📈 Impacts loan-to-value calculations
  • 🛡️ Affects negative equity protection needs

Key Assumptions in ERM Calculations

🏠 Property Growth

Annual appreciation rate projections (typically 2-4%)

💸 Interest Rates

Fixed vs variable rate scenarios

📉 Market Risks

Economic downturn probabilities

⚕️ Health Factors

Early care home admission probabilities

📊 Example Scenario

Borrower Age: 70
Property Value: £400,000
Interest Rate: 5% fixed
Projected Longevity: 85 years
Outcome: Loan balance doubles every 14 years

Important Considerations

  • 🔍 Always consult a financial advisor
  • 📑 Review terms carefully
  • ⚖️ Compare multiple providers
  • 🛡️ Verify consumer protections
